<h1>TTRPG Dice Roller app for the Badgerware Tufty 2350</h1>

<h2>Features</h2>
This is a small app that allows for rolling dice of various common sizes and custom quantities, all whilst displaying a colorful wire-grid representation of specific dice in the background.

If more than one die is rolled at once, each die will be displayed individually at the top with the total below.
The last previously rolled total is displayed below the current total.
The currently selected dice size and quantity are shown at the bottom.

<h2>Controls</h2>
<h3>Dice Size</h3>
Pressing A will decrease size.
Pressing B will increase size.
<h3>Dice pool quantity</h3>
Pressing UP (^) will increase quantity.
Pressing DOWN (v) will decrease quantity.
<h3>Roll dice pool</h3>
Pressing B will roll the dice pool
<h3>Reset to 1d20 shortcut</h3>
Pressing and holding B will reset the dice pool to 1x d20

<h2>Installation</h2>
Simply drag and drop the 'dice_roller' folder and its contents into the 'apps' folder on your Tufty 2350 and launch it from BadgeOS

<h3>Requirements</h3>
A Tufty 2350 and BadgeOS v4.03 (untested with future versions)
